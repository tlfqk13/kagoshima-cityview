The service is up and running.

Accurate GPS coordinates for all 20 bus stops. Korean, English, and Japanese. A PWA that works even offline. The bus stops where Google Maps gets it wrong, marked.

The next step is sending a proposal to Kagoshima City's tourism division (観光課).

The proposal:
1. Link to this service from the official tourism website
2. Add this service's URL to the QR codes on bus stop signage
3. Cooperate on syncing when GTFS data is updated

This is a service one solo developer built in a few days. It's not perfect. But it targets the problem precisely.

If one traveler's inconvenience can become the catalyst for improving the whole city's tourism experience, that's enough.
