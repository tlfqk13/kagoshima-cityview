Back at the hotel, I did some searching.

"kagoshima city view bus stop wrong location"

The English TripAdvisor had reviews. "GPS showed wrong location, missed the bus." Korean travel blogs had similar stories. And in Japanese reviews, posts like "バス停の位置がずれている" ("the bus stop location is off") kept showing up.

They all had one thing in common: the City View bus stop locations on Google Maps didn't match reality.

The worst case was the Tenmonkan bus stop. It sits in the busiest shopping district, the one tourists use the most — and yet it had the largest coordinate error.

This wasn't one person's mistake. It was a system problem.
